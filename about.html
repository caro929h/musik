WHAT IS CLASSICROOTS?
At ClassicRoots, we believe that R&B music is not just a genre, but a lifestyle. Our platform is dedicated to celebrating the rich history and soulful sounds of R&B, while also providing a space for fans to connect and share their passion for the music. Our platform features a library of classic R&B tracks, from smooth slow jams to upbeat dance tunes.
ClassicRoots is more than just a music platform - it’s a community. Our forums and social media channels provide a space for fans and artists to share their thoughts, ideas, and experiences related to R&B music. Whether you want to discuss the latest album from your favorite artist, share your favorite song, or connect with like-minded fans from around the world, you’ll find a welcoming and supportive community at ClassicRoots.
